How it works?

Make your life easier and save time, money and pain.

  • 1 . White-label plugin.

    Our plugin is easy to install and fully customisable. Influencers will not notice the difference when they make requests, because it looks and feel like your website.

  • 2 . Clear your schedule.

    Enough sorting through old emails trying to find that "amazing influencer" who you were emailing last week but whose name you can't recall. Your communications with them can be easily sorted out for you.

  • 3 . Filter your requests.

    All the data on the influencers is listed. Your top picks will be shown, and you can filter depending on your requirements at any given time.

  • 4 . Big data.

    With #YesFilter technology you will even get recommendations of influencers that have not made a request but they are the perfect fit for your needs. Our algorithm can make suggestions and give you the best ROI for your money.

  • 5. Follow up and calculate your ROI.

    Our tool also allows you to easily follow up on how your investment is bringing money back to your hotel. You can track and measure your results without having to chase all the social networks.
